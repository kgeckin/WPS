What is phishing?
Phishing is a social engineering attack where cybercriminals trick users into sharing sensitive information (such as passwords) by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

How to protect yourself:

  • Always check the sender and the link address before clicking.
  • Do not enter your credentials into suspicious or unexpected pages.
  • Report suspicious messages to your IT/security team.
  • When in doubt, verify the sender through another channel.
